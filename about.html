This website

I purposely designed this website to feel like you're flipping through pages of a portfolio. I feel that modern websites (the ones where you only scroll down) are actually quite difficult to get around. The more content you try to fit onto a page, the more likely your audience will be lost. The more flair you add to a page, the more confusing things become. Every ounce of free time is precious.


I also break the textAdding a full space between chunks of text. pretty often. Here's why:

When reading, peoples' eyes are constantly looking for something to anchor to. It gives the reader a sense of stability. If you stare at a wall of text, you're probably going to lose track of which line you were reading. I know because I'm one of those people. I love learning, but I absolutely hate reading. But when I started writing posts for my own website, I realized that this problem could be solved simply by breaking the text.


This website is designed with the above concepts in mind. For your convenience, here are a couple of the features employed in my website:


  • This website is designed to display properly with any dimension.
  • Text in orange are always links. Links taking you outside of this website will always be opened in a new window.
  • Text with dotted underlinesA dotted line below a text. are tooltips. Hover over them to read what they mean. I understand that most people don't want to google every new term, so don't worry! I'll do the explaining.
  • There are a lot of bolded text. For people who get lost in text easily, these are your anchors.
